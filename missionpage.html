Home

Teens and young women often feel pressured to fit into a societal mold, one that forces them to look a certain way in order to be considered pretty and successful. These societal norms undermine their self-confidence and the need to look 'perfect' envokes insecurities and unnecessary stress. Through Moda, we want to teach girls to love their bodies and show them how to style themselves to fit their body shape, hence increasing their poise and self-assurance. We hope you enjoy our site!
