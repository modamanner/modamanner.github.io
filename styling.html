The Different Body Types

If you have a Column Body Shape, then you have: Narrow Shoulders,Flat Chest or Small Bust, Small and non-defined waist, Narrow Hips and Flat Bottom

If you have a Pear Body Shape, then you have: Full hips/thighs, A Defined Waist, Shoulders that are Narrower than your Hips, A small Top Half as well as Bust

If you have a Neat Hourglass Body Shape, then you have: A Defined Bust, A Defined Waist, and Defined Hips

If you have an Inverted Triangle Body Shape, then you have: Flat Hips, Your Bottom Half is smaller than your Top Half, your Shoulders are Straight and Squared, and very little Definition between your Waist and Hips

If you have an Apple Body Shape, then you have: Rounder Shoulders, Average to Big Bust, and Fullness around the Middle
