The Different Body Types

For more detailed information, click on the body shape.

If you have a column body shape, then you have: narrow shoulders, flat chest or small bust, small and non-defined waist, narrow hips, and a flat bottom.

If you have a pear body shape, then you have: full hips/thighs, a defined waist, shoulders that are narrower than your hips, and a small top half or bust.

If you have an hourglass body shape, then you have: a defined bust, waist, and hips.

If you have an inverted triangle body shape, then you have: flat hips and very little definition between your waist and hips, your bottom half is smaller than your top half, and your shoulders are straight and squared.

If you have an apple body shape, then you have: rounder shoulders, average/big bust, and fullness around the middle.
